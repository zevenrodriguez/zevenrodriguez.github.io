Bio

Zevensuy Rodriguez is a maker/designer/prototyper born in Miami, FL, He is currently a lecturer in the Department of Cinema and Interactive Media at the University of Miami. He graduated and was a Fellow at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Masters Program and did his undergrad at Univ. of Miami’s Visual Communications program. He founded Howlin' Wolves a prototyping and fabrication company based in Brooklyn. Projects range from web, electronics, to custom fabrication including CNC Milling. His research focuses on user’s interaction with internet objects by creating interesting interfaces. He also explores combining traditional fabrication techniques with digital ones.
